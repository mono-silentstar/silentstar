## Final Elixir Formula (9 Vials)

### Ingredients Per Vial (30ml serving)

**Active compounds:**
- L-arginine: 3g (4 capsules × 750mg)
- Damiana: 1.33g (1.33 capsules × 1000mg)
- Horny goat weed: 1.5g equivalent (1 capsule: 50mg 20:1 extract + 500mg regular)

**Liquid base per vial:**
- ~20-25ml prostatic fluid (pure base, frozen in advance)
- ~5ml additional liquid if needed for volume
- Rose water (~1/2 tsp)
- Cardamom (crushed or extract, small amount)
- Glycerin (1 tsp for thickness and lingering mouthfeel)
- Honey (1-2 tsp for sweetness, helps suspend powders, coats tongue)
- Ginger (small amount for heat/intensity) - optional
- Edible glitter (gold or iridescent) - optional for visual ritual element

### Total Batch Ingredients (9 vials)
- 36 L-arginine capsules (750mg each)
- 12 damiana capsules (1000mg each)  
- 9 horny goat weed capsules
- ~200ml+ prostatic fluid (collected and frozen from previous sessions)
- Honey, rose water, cardamom, glycerin, ginger, glitter as needed

**Preparation:**
- Break open capsules, dump powder into liquid base
- Mix thoroughly (will be thick, slightly gritty from binders)
- Divide into 9 vials
- Refrigerate, use within 5-7 days
- Shake vigorously before each use

**Taste profile:** Sweet-heat-herbal-bitter with lingering honey coating and ginger burn

**Color:** Golden-brown shimmer, opaque, thick

## Safety Decisions - Plurality Protection

**DROPPED from formula:**
- **Maca (Lepidium meyenii):** Increases BDNF (brain-derived neurotrophic factor), promotes neuroplasticity and neurogenesis - risk to plurality
- **Muira puama (Ptychopetalum olacoides):** Potentiates NGF-mediated neurite outgrowth (same mechanism that suppressed plurality with lion's mane) - CRITICAL RISK

Both dropped despite potential aphrodisiac benefits to protect plurality. Final formula uses only ingredients with peripheral mechanisms (blood flow, PDE5 inhibition, relaxation).

**Essential oils NOT safe for ingestion:**
- Do NOT put essential oil blend into elixir
- Most oils (tuberose, jasmine absolutes, ylang ylang, clary sage) are NOT food-safe
- Would cause mouth/throat burn, GI distress
- Keep scent and taste as SEPARATE triggers for conditioning
