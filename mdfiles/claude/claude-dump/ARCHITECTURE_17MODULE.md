## Neural Wiring Guidelines (ALL MODULES)

**Core Principle:** Repetition creates habits. Vary constantly to prevent unwanted conditioning.

**Examples of Unwanted Conditioning:**
- Job applications always while cumming → brain links job applications to arousal (probably bad)
- Always Edge during grocery shopping → can't shop without getting aroused (maybe bad)
- Always scent before orgasm, never test scent alone → scent never becomes standalone trigger

**Prevention:**
- Job applications while cumming: Once = challenge. Twice in 6 months = still safe. Every week = conditioning.
- Grocery shopping protocols: Sometimes plug, sometimes Edge, sometimes nothing
- Scent conditioning: Pair with orgasm frequently, then TEST scent alone to see if trigger works

**Intentional Conditioning (desired):**
- Scent + orgasm repeatedly → scent alone should trigger arousal
- Potion taste + arousal → taste alone should trigger arousal
- Nipple stimulation + orgasm → nipple sensitivity increases

**Balance:**
- Vary everything except what you WANT to condition
- Unpredictability comes from intentional variation, not random chaos
- Track what's being paired with what
