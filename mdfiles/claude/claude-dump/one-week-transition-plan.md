# One-Week Comfort Transition Plan

**Purpose:** Breakup recovery food plan. Low stress, gradual ramp-up from convenience to batch cooking. Transition to 3-week meal plan by Day 8.

---

## The Arc

- **Days 1-3:** Minimal cooking, canned convenience, getting oriented
- **Days 4-5:** Start batching components
- **Days 6-7:** Coasting on batched stuff, kitchen clean
- **Day 8+:** Transition to 3-week meal plan

---

## Day 1 (Before Shopping)

Handle however - sandwich, frozen meal, takeaway, or emergency rice with stock cube.

---

## Shop 1 (Day 2 Morning - M&S/Sainsbury's)

### Convenience
- Instant noodles: 5 packs
- Canned soup: 4 tins (chicken, mushroom, tomato - your choice)

### Fresh Veg
- Carrots: 1kg (6-8 carrots)
- Tomatoes: 6-pack
- Mushrooms: 250g pack
- Cabbage: 1 whole
- Green onions: 2 bunches
- Celery: 1 head (optional)

### Protein & Dairy
- Eggs: 12

### Pantry
- Dried noodles: 2 packs (not instant)
- Rice vinegar (if you don't have - for marinated eggs later)

### Check You Have
Rice, stock cubes, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, cooking oil

---

## Shop 2 (Day 4 Morning)

### Protein
- Minced beef: 500g

### Fresh
- Eggs: 12
- Bean sprouts: 1 pack (optional noodle topping)

### Check Pantry
More dried noodles if running low, stock cubes

### Day 4 (Start Batching)

**Meals:**
- **Breakfast:** Instant noodles + 2 fresh eggs
- **Lunch:** Canned soup + rice
- **Dinner:** Vegetable soup + 1 egg dropped in

**Evening Batch Prep:**
1. Soft-boil 12 eggs → cool → marinate in: 3 parts soy sauce, 1 part water, splash mirin/sugar
2. Blanch bean sprouts + any extra cabbage scraps for noodle toppings → store in fridge
3. Optional: Make aromatic oil (3 parts neutral oil, 1 part vinegar, chopped green onion) → store in jar

---

### Day 5

**Meals:**
- **Breakfast:** Dried noodles + stock cube + 2 marinated eggs + green onion
- **Lunch:** Minced beef over rice (1st portion) + 2 marinated eggs on side
- **Dinner:** Vegetable soup + 1 egg dropped in

**Evening Batch Prep:**
- Cook all 500g minced beef: brown, season with soy sauce, ginger, garlic. Throw in any leftover carrots, cabbage scraps, mushroom bits. Portion into 4 containers.

## Vegetable Soup Recipe

**Same for all 6 dinners (Days 2-7)**

### Ingredients (Per Dinner)
- 1 carrot (chopped)
- 1 tomato (chopped)
- Handful mushrooms (~2-3 mushrooms, sliced)
- Chunk of cabbage (~1/6 of head, chopped)
- Bit of celery (if you got it)
- 1 stock cube
- Water to cover
- Soy sauce to taste
- 1 egg dropped in at the end

### Method
Chuck everything except egg in pot, bring to boil, simmer 15-20 mins until veg soft. Drop egg in, stir gently, done.

### Total Veg Used Across 6 Dinners
- 6 carrots
- 6 tomatoes
- Most of mushroom pack
- Most of cabbage
- Most celery

**Leftover veg for beef:** Whatever extra carrots from the bag, cabbage scraps, mushroom bits - chuck it all in when you batch cook the beef on Day 5.

- 1 cup uncooked rice per portion (cook fresh each time)
